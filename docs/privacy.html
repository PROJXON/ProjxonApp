Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-03

This app (“Projxon”) is a portfolio project. We collect only the data needed to provide chat, media attachments, notifications, and account features.

Data we collect

  • Account data: username/display name and identifiers from authentication (Cognito).
  • Chat content: messages you send and metadata (timestamps, conversation ids).
  • Media: images/videos/files you upload as message attachments and avatar images.
  • Push tokens: device push token for direct message notifications.

How we use data

  • Provide messaging features (send/receive, edit/delete your messages, reactions).
  • Provide blocking and safety features (blocklist, report/flag).
  • Provide direct message notifications (push tokens).

AI features

If you use the AI helper features, message history and optional attachment thumbnails may be sent to an AI provider to generate responses. Use of this feature is optional.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data with service providers needed to operate the app (e.g., hosting, storage, authentication, notifications, AI provider if used).

Data retention

We keep account and content data until you delete it, delete your account, or as needed for safety/legal reasons. Some content may remain visible to other users (for example, messages already delivered) unless removed.

Delete your account

You can delete your account from within the app. You can also use the web deletion link: Delete Account.

Timeline: account deletion is typically immediate, but some cleanup (such as cached media) may take a short time to fully disappear.
